About Praetor

Praetor is a modern practice platform for developers who want to sharpen their problem-solving skills in a focused, low-friction environment.

What you can do

Join contests, solve curated problems, review submissions, and track progress over time.

How it works

Create an account, sign in, and start exploring problem sets and challenge rounds.

Need help?

Use the login and registration routes or contact the team once the backend is running locally.
